## Adding Players

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Click "Add Player"** button in the squad builder
2. **Fill in the required fields:**

   **Name** (Required)
   - Enter the player's full name
   - Example: "Cristiano Ronaldo"

   **Overall Rating (OVR)** (Required)
   - Range: 1-99
   - This represents the player's overall ability
   - Example: 85 for a top wonderkid

   **Age** (Required)
   - Range: 15-40
   - Focus on wonderkids (17-23) for career mode
   - Example: 19

   **Position** (Required)
   - Select from dropdown
   - Options: GK, CB, LB, RB, DMF, CMF, AMF, LWF, RWF, CF, SS
   - Example: CF (Center Forward)

   **Playing Style** (Required)
   - Automatically filtered based on position
   - Choose the style that matches the player
   - Example: "Goal Poacher" for a clinical striker

   **Original Club** (Optional)
   - The club the player came from
   - Example: "Manchester United"

3. **Click "Add Player"** to save

### Playing Styles Explained

#### Goalkeepers (GK)
- **Offensive Goalkeeper**: Sweeper keeper, good with feet
- **Defensive Goalkeeper**: Traditional shot-stopper

#### Center Backs (CB)
- **Extra Frontman**: Joins attacks, scores from set pieces
- **Build Up**: Good passer, starts attacks from back
- **Destroyer**: Aggressive tackler, wins duels
- **The Wall**: Defensive rock, blocks everything

#### Full Backs (LB/RB)
- **Offensive Fullback**: Overlaps wingers, provides width
- **Defensive Fullback**: Stays back, solid defender
- **Roaming Flank**: Versatile, moves inside and wide

#### Defensive Midfielders (DMF)
- **Anchor Man**: Sits deep, shields defense
- **Destroyer**: Aggressive ball winner
- **Orchestrator**: Deep-lying playmaker
- **Box-to-Box**: Covers entire midfield

#### Central Midfielders (CMF)
- **Box-to-Box**: High energy, attacks and defends
- **Hole Player**: Makes late runs into box
- **Orchestrator**: Controls tempo, dictates play
- **The Destroyer**: Defensive midfielder

#### Attacking Midfielders (AMF)
- **Classic No. 10**: Traditional playmaker
- **Hole Player**: Finds space between lines
- **Creative Playmaker**: Creates chances
- **Box-to-Box**: All-action midfielder

#### Wingers (LWF/RWF)
- **Prolific Winger**: Pace and dribbling, beats defenders
- **Roaming Flank**: Cuts inside, versatile
- **Goal Poacher**: Finisher from wide areas

#### Strikers (CF)
- **Goal Poacher**: Clinical finisher, right place right time
- **Fox In The Box**: Penalty box predator
- **Advanced Striker**: Complete forward
- **Target Man**: Hold-up play, wins headers

#### Second Strikers (SS)
- **Creative Playmaker**: Links midfield and attack
- **Hole Player**: Finds pockets of space
- **Dummy Runner**: Creates space for others

## Managing Your Squad

### Viewing Players
- All players displayed as cards
- Shows: Name, OVR, Age, Position, Playing Style, Club
- Sorted by most recently added

### Searching Players
- Use search box to find players by name
- Real-time filtering as you type
- Case-insensitive search

### Filtering by Position
- Use position dropdown to filter
- Select "All Positions" to see everyone
- Useful for checking depth in each position

### Editing Players
1. Click "Edit" button on player card
2. Modify any field
3. Click "Update Player"
4. Changes saved immediately

### Deleting Players
1. Click "Delete" button on player card
2. Confirm deletion in popup
3. Player removed permanently

### Quick Stats Panel
- **Total Players**: Your squad size
- **Average OVR**: Squad quality indicator
- **Average Age**: Squad age profile
- **Progress Indicator**: Shows when you can generate recommendations

## Generating Recommendations

### Requirements
- **Minimum 11 players** in your squad
- Recommended: 15-20 players for better analysis
- Include players in all positions for balanced recommendations

### How to Generate
1. Add at least 11 players to your squad
2. Click **"Get AI Recommendations"** button (top right)
3. Wait 2-3 seconds for AI analysis
4. View comprehensive tactical recommendations

### What Gets Analyzed
The AI considers:
- Player overall ratings
- Position compatibility
- Playing style synergies
- Age and potential
- Formation optimization
- Squad balance

## Tips & Best Practices

### Building Your Squad

**1. Balance is Key**
- Don't just add attackers
- Ensure coverage in all positions
- Have backup players (15-20 total)

**2. Age Considerations**
- Focus on wonderkids (17-23) for career mode
- Mix of ages for immediate impact and future potential
- Younger players get priority in AI recommendations

**3. Playing Style Diversity**
- Vary playing styles for tactical flexibility
- Example: Have both "Goal Poacher" and "Target Man" strikers
- Allows different approaches based on opponent

**4. OVR Distribution**
- Aim for average OVR of 75-80 for competitive squad
- Have some star players (85+) as game-changers
- Don't neglect lower-rated players with potential

### Using Recommendations

**1. Starting XI**
- Trust the AI's position assignments
- Note the tactical explanations
- Star players (‚≠ê) are your key performers

**2. Super Subs**
- Follow timing recommendations
- Use when match situation matches advice
- Fresh legs can change games

**3. Formations**
- Start with balanced formation
- Switch to offensive if losing after 60 minutes
- Switch to defensive if winning after 75 minutes

**4. Tactical Strategies**
- Read strategies before matches
- Identify opponent style early
- Adjust tactics accordingly
- Use key players in recommended roles

### Career Mode Integration

**1. Transfer Planning**
- Use recommendations to identify weak positions
- Look for players with recommended playing styles
- Balance immediate needs with future potential

**2. Training Focus**
- Train key players identified by AI
- Develop super subs for future starting roles
- Focus on positions lacking depth

**3. Match Preparation**
- Review recommendations before big matches
- Prepare formation changes in advance
- Know your super sub options

**4. Season Planning**
- Generate new recommendations after transfers
- Adjust tactics as players develop
- Re-evaluate every transfer window

## FAQ

**Q: How many players do I need?**
A: Minimum 11 to generate recommendations, but 15-20 is ideal for better analysis and depth.

**Q: Can I save multiple squads?**
A: Currently, the app works with one squad at a time. Future updates will add multi-squad support.

**Q: Why isn't my best player in the starting XI?**
A: The AI considers position fit, playing style compatibility, and formation optimization, not just OVR.

**Q: Can I manually adjust the starting XI?**
A: Currently, the AI generates the lineup. Manual editing is planned for future updates.

**Q: How often should I regenerate recommendations?**
A: After adding/removing players, or when you want to try different tactical approaches.

**Q: What if I disagree with a recommendation?**
A: The AI provides suggestions based on data. Use your football knowledge and preferences to adapt.

**Q: Can I use this for real-life football?**
A: While designed for career mode, the tactical principles apply to real football management.

**Q: Does the AI learn from my preferences?**
A: Currently no, but personalized recommendations are planned for future updates.

**Q: Can I share my squad with friends?**
A: Use the text export feature to share. URL sharing is coming in future updates.

**Q: What formations does the AI support?**
A: 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 4-2-3-1, 3-5-2, 5-3-2, 4-2-4, and 3-4-3.

**Q: How accurate are the recommendations?**
A: Based on proven tactical principles and playing style synergies. Results may vary based on your gameplay.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: No, requires internet connection for database access. Offline mode planned for future.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Can't add player**
- Check all required fields are filled
- Verify OVR is 1-99
- Verify age is 15-40
- Ensure playing style matches position

**Problem: Recommendations not generating**
- Ensure you have at least 11 players
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page and try again
- Check browser console for errors

**Problem: Export not working**
- Check browser allows downloads
- Try different browser
- Ensure popup blocker isn't interfering

**Problem: Players not showing**
- Refresh the page
- Check search/filter settings
- Verify database connection
